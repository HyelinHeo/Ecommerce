● What's volume weight

·Volume weight refers to the area occupied by the shipment converted into weight.
·Rates are set based on the heavy side by comparing volume weight and actual weight.


● Volume Weight Translation Method

·Volume weight is calculated by the following formula:


·For air transportation (including special delivery)



(width * height * length)(cm)

5000


·In the case of sea transportation



(width * height * length)(cm)

6000
